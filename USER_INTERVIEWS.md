# SpendPilot AI — User Interview Summaries

To validate the product, we conducted mock interviews with 3 target users.

## 👤 User 1: Founder at Seed-stage Fintech (12 employees)
- **Pain Point**: "I have no idea who has a ChatGPT Plus account and who is using the team plan. I think we're paying for both."
- **Feedback on SpendPilot**: "The 'Consolidate to single AI IDE' recommendation alone saved us $150/mo. The terminal loading screen made me feel like it actually scanned our docs."
- **Key Feature Request**: "Can you automatically cancel the subscriptions for me?"

## 👤 User 2: Head of Engineering at Series-A SaaS (45 employees)
- **Pain Point**: "Our OpenAI API bill jumped from $500 to $4,000 in one month. We don't have time to audit every prompt."
- **Feedback on SpendPilot**: "The 'Growth Capital Forecast' is what I'll show my CEO. It turns a boring cost-cutting exercise into a 'how to hire another engineer' conversation."
- **Key Feature Request**: "Slack alerts when a new tool is added to the stack."

## 👤 User 3: Fractional CFO (Working with 8 startups)
- **Pain Point**: "I spend hours every month manually auditing SaaS spend for my clients."
- **Feedback on SpendPilot**: "I would pay for this just to have a clean 'Efficiency Score' I can put in my monthly board reports."
- **Key Feature Request**: "White-label reports so I can put my own logo on it."

## 💡 Key Synthesized Insights
1.  **Trust is emotional**: The "Analysis" phase is critical for the user to value the output.
2.  **Runway is the metric**: Founders don't care about $1,000; they care about 0.5 months of extra survival.
3.  **Categories matter**: Consolidation (Redundancy) is the #1 value prop.
