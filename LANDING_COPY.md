# SpendPilot AI — Landing Page Copy Strategy

This document outlines the conversion-focused copy strategy for the landing page.

## 📐 The Formula
We follow the **P.A.S. (Problem, Agitation, Solution)** framework.

### 1. The Hero (The Solution)
- **Headline**: Your AI Stack Is Probably Overpriced.
- **Sub-headline**: Analyze your AI tooling spend, uncover hidden waste, and discover smarter alternatives — in under 60 seconds.
- **CTA**: Run Free Audit.
- **Micro-copy**: No login required. No credit card needed.

### 2. The Agitation (The Waste)
- **Copy**: "Are you paying for Cursor *and* Copilot? ChatGPT Team *and* 5 individual Plus accounts? Your growth capital is leaking into redundant licenses."
- **Visual**: Bento grid showing tool overlaps.

### 3. The Process (How it Works)
1.  **Configure**: Add your tools and seat counts.
2.  **Analyze**: Our engine runs 50+ benchmark comparisons.
3.  **Optimize**: Get a prioritized roadmap to reclaim your budget.

### 4. The Result (The Value)
- **Copy**: "Not just savings. Growth capital."
- **Counter**: Live ticker showing total $ saved by all SpendPilot users.

## ✍️ Tone of Voice
- **Authoritative**: We know the pricing better than the vendors do.
- **Start-up Focused**: We speak "Founder" (runway, capital efficiency, burn rate).
- **Premium**: Minimalist, clean, and financially intelligent.
