# SpendPilot AI — Success Metrics

How we measure the success and health of the SpendPilot AI platform.

## 📈 North Star Metric
- **Total Identified Savings**: The aggregate dollar amount SpendPilot has found for its users. This is our primary value indicator.

## 📊 Core KPI Dashboard

### 1. User Acquisition & Growth
- **Viral Coefficient (k)**: Number of new users generated by one public report share. (Target: > 0.2).
- **Report Share Rate**: % of users who reach the results page and click "Share Report." (Target: > 15%).

### 2. Engagement
- **Audit Completion Rate**: % of users who start the `/audit` flow and reach `/results`. (Target: > 75%).
- **Time to Value (TTV)**: Seconds from landing page to seeing the first $ savings identified. (Target: < 90 seconds).

### 3. Conversion
- **Lead Capture Rate**: % of results page viewers who enter their email for the "Full Roadmap." (Target: > 10%).

### 4. Technical Performance
- **Lighthouse Performance Score**: Target > 90.
- **API Latency**: Average response time for `/api/audit`. (Target: < 200ms).

## 📅 Tracking Plan
- **Analytics**: Vercel Analytics for page views and speed.
- **Events**: Custom event tracking on "Run Audit" and "Get Roadmap" buttons.
- **Reporting**: Weekly automated digest of new leads and total savings identified.
