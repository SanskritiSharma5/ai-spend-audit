# SpendPilot AI — Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

This document outlines how SpendPilot AI would be launched and scaled in the real world.

## 🎯 Target Audience
1.  **Seed/Series-A Founders**: Who need to extend runway but lack a dedicated procurement team.
2.  **Fractional CFOs**: Who can use SpendPilot as a tool to show immediate value to their clients.
3.  **Venture Capitalists (VCs)**: Who can distribute the tool to their entire portfolio to improve aggregate capital efficiency.

## 📈 Distribution Channels
- **Product-Led Growth (PLG)**: The "Public Shareable Report" is our primary growth loop. When a founder shares their savings on LinkedIn/Twitter, it drives new users to "Run their own audit."
- **Partnerships**: Partner with startup ecosystems (Y Combinator, Techstars) and accounting software (Quickbooks, Xero) for API-based integrations.
- **Content Marketing**: "The State of AI Spend 2024" — a report generated from anonymous aggregate data from SpendPilot audits.

## 💰 Monetization
- **Freemium**: Basic audit is free (current MVP).
- **Pro ($49/mo)**: Unlimited audits, employee usage tracking, and automated "Price Drop" alerts.
- **Enterprise (2.5% of savings)**: White-glove procurement service where SpendPilot negotiates directly with vendors on behalf of the company.

## 🏆 Competitive Advantage
Most spend management tools (Ramp, Brex) focus on *tracking* spend. SpendPilot focuses on **optimizing** spend specifically for the AI era, where seat-based licensing and API credits are the primary waste areas.
