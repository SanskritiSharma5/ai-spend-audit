# SpendPilot AI — Unit Economics

An analysis of the cost vs. value of the SpendPilot AI platform.

## 💵 Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- **Average Monthly Savings Identified**: $1,400 (per Seed-stage company).
- **Pro Subscription**: $49/mo.
- **Estimated Retention**: 18 months (foundations of procurement are established in this window).
- **Projected LTV**: ~$882.

## 📉 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Primary Channel**: Organic social shares (OG images) + Content Marketing.
- **Estimated CAC**: $12.50 (highly efficient due to the "Shareable Savings" loop).

## 📊 LTV/CAC Ratio
- **Ratio**: ~70x.
- This high ratio is driven by the viral nature of "finding money" and the low infrastructure cost of the platform.

## 🏗️ Infrastructure Costs (COGS)
- **Hosting**: $0 (Vercel free tier for initial scale).
- **Database**: $0 (Supabase free tier).
- **AI Inference**: $0.05 per audit (GPT-4o mini for summaries).
- **Total Variable Cost**: < $0.10 per customer per month.

## 💡 Summary
SpendPilot AI is a high-margin software business with a massive negative churn potential — as a startup grows, their AI spend increases, making SpendPilot's optimization more valuable over time.
